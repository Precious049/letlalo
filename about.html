About Us

Our Story, Our Culture, Our Pride

Who We Are

Letlalo is a proudly Motswana fashion brand rooted in the traditions and identity of the Tswana people. The name Letlalo, meaning skin in Setswana, represents the very essence of who we are — our identity, our heritage and our pride.

Founded by Precious Phelelo, Letlalo was born from a burning desire to keep Tswana culture alive through fashion. Growing up surrounded by the beauty of Botswana's traditions, Precious saw a generation slowly drifting away from its roots and ancestors. Letlalo was her answer — a brand that dresses the body while anchoring the soul to its origins.
